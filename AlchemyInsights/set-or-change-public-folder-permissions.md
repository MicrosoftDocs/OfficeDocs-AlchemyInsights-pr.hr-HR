# <a name="permissions-and-public-folders"></a>Dozvole i javne mape

Dozvole za javne mape možete promijeniti pomoću programa Outlook, centra za administratore sustava Exchange (EAC) ili ljuske PowerShell:
  
- Za upute za Outlook [kliknite ovdje](https://support.office.com/article/Set-or-change-permissions-for-a-public-folder-b2e0440c-7873-48ec-9ff2-b1a20b723005.aspx).
    
- Upute potražite u [ovom članku.](https://technet.microsoft.com/library/jj651147%28v=exchg.150%29.aspx.aspx#Anchor_1) 
    
- Upute za korištenje naredbe Add-PublicFolderClientPermission potražite u [ovom članku.](https://technet.microsoft.com/library/bb124743%28v=exchg.160%29.aspx.aspx) Ako trebate upute za spajanje na Exchange PowerShell, kliknite [ovdje](https://technet.microsoft.com/library/jj984289%28v=exchg.160%29.aspx.aspx).
    
Ako **vanjski korisnici ne mogu slati poruke e-pošte javnoj mapi omogućenoj za poštu,** razlog može biti taj što javna mapa nema dozvole potrebne za vanjsku isporuku e-pošte. To možete popraviti pomoću [ovdje](https://technet.microsoft.com/library/aa997560%28v=exchg.150%29.aspx.aspx#Anchor_1)navedenih uputa programa Outlook ili [uputa](https://support.microsoft.com/help/2984402/-5.7.1-smtp-550-5.7.1-resolver.rst.authrequired-nondelivery-report-when-external-users-try-to-send-mail-to-mail-enabled-public-folders-in-office-365.aspx)komponente PowerShell .
