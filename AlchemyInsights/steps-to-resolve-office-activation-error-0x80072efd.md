# <a name="steps-to-resolve-office-activation-error-0x80072efd"></a>Koraci za rješavanje Office activation pogreška 0x80072EFD


Isprobajte navedena rješenja da biste ispravili pogrešku aktivaciju paketa Office:
  
1. Provjerite [vatrozid](https://support.office.com/article/0d23d3c0-c19c-4b2f-9845-5344fedc4380#BKMK_CheckFirewall).
    
2. Provjerite svoj [protuvirusni softver](https://support.office.com/article/0d23d3c0-c19c-4b2f-9845-5344fedc4380#BKMK_CheckAV).
    
3. Provjerite [Postavke proxy poslužitelja](https://support.office.com/article/0d23d3c0-c19c-4b2f-9845-5344fedc4380#BKMK_CheckProxy)
    
4. Preuzmite i instalirajte [podršku i oporavak pomoćnika](https://aka.ms/SARA-OfficeActivation-Alchemy) i pokrenite **Aktivaciju za otklanjanje poteškoća**.
