> [!NOTE]
> <span data-ttu-id="ad8d4-p101">Mogućnost platiti fakturu dostupna samo ako pretplatu Office 365 troškove preko određenim iznosom (koji se razlikuje za mjesto servisa). 1. <span data-ttu-id="ad8d4-106">U centru za administraciju idite na stranicu [pretplate](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=842054) ili odaberite **naplate** \> **pretplate**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad8d4-106">In the Admin Center, go to the [Subscriptions](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=842054) page, or choose **Billing** \> **Subscriptions**.</span></span>
    
2. <span data-ttu-id="ad8d4-p102">Odaberite pretplate koje želite promijeniti. 4. <span data-ttu-id="ad8d4-112">U u \*\* promijeniti način plaćanja \*\* dijaloški okvir, odaberite **Nastavi**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad8d4-112">In the \*\* Change your payment method \*\* dialog box, choose **Continue**.</span></span>
