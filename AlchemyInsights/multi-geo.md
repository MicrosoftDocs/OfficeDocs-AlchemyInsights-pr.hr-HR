- [Multi-Geo mogućnosti u servisu OneDrive i SharePoint Online](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/enterprise/multi-geo-capabilities-in-onedrive-and-sharepoint-online-in-office-365)
- [Multi-Geo mogućnosti u sustavu Exchange Online](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/enterprise/multi-geo-capabilities-in-exchange-online)
- [Plan za Office 365 multi-Geo](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/enterprise/plan-for-multi-geo)
- [Konfiguriranje multi-Geo](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/enterprise/multi-geo-tenant-configuration)
