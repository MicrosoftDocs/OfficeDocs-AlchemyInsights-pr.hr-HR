# <a name="get-phone-numbers"></a>Dohvaćanje telefonskih brojeva

Kada kupite [telefonski sustav i nazovete licence za tarifu](https://docs.microsoft.com/MicrosoftTeams/setting-up-your-phone-system#step-2-buy-and-assign-phone-system-and-calling-plan-licenses), morat ćete nabaviti telefonske brojeve za svoje korisnike. Imate 3 načina za dohvaćanje telefonskih brojeva.

1. [Nabavite nove brojeve pomoću centra za administratore timova](https://docs.microsoft.com/MicrosoftTeams/setting-up-your-phone-system#get-new-user-phone-numbers-using-the-teams-admin-center).
2. [Nabavite nove brojeve koji nisu dostupni u centru za administratore timova](https://docs.microsoft.com/MicrosoftTeams/setting-up-your-phone-system#get-new-numbers-that-arent-available-in-the-teams-admin-center).
3. [Priključke ili prijenos telefonskih brojeva davatelja usluga ili telefonskog operatera](https://docs.microsoft.com/MicrosoftTeams/setting-up-your-phone-system#port-or-transfer-phone-numbers-from-your-service-provider-or-phone-carrier).

Dodatne informacije potražite u članku [dohvaćanje telefonskih brojeva za korisnike](https://docs.microsoft.com/MicrosoftTeams/setting-up-your-phone-system#port-or-transfer-phone-numbers-from-your-service-provider-or-phone-carrier).
