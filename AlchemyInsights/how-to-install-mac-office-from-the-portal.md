# <a name="how-to-install-mac-office-from-the-portal"></a>Kako instalirati Mac Office s portala

1. [Deinstalirajte Office 2011 za Mac s računala](https://support.office.com/article/4bfcd230-0ea1-4656-bf30-dbfa44d358fa?wt.mc_id=Alchemy_ClientDIA).

2. [Upravljajte postavkama preuzimanja softvera u sustavu Office 365](https://docs.microsoft.com/DeployOffice/manage-software-download-settings-office-365).

3. [Dodijelite licence korisnicima](https://docs.microsoft.com//office365/admin/subscriptions-and-billing/assign-licenses-to-users).

4. Korisnici tada mogu sami instalirati softver sustava Office ili možete koristiti [Office za implementaciju na svoje Mac korisnike](https://docs.microsoft.com/DeployOffice/mac/deployment-guide-for-office-for-mac).
