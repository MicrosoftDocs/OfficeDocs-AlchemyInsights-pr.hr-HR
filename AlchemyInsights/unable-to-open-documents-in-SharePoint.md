Dodatne informacije ili pojedinosti potražite [u okviru rješavanje problema s otvaranjem dokumenata u SharePoint bibliotekama](https://support.office.com/article/Fix-problems-opening-documents-in-SharePoint-libraries-31329FA1-4AD0-47FC-95D8-BB0C5B12A536).
