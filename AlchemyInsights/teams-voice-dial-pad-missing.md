Ako vaša pločica za biranje nedostaje u Microsoftovim timovima, molimo provjerite sljedeće:

- Provjerite je li korisniku dodijeljena [licenca timova](https://docs.microsoft.com/MicrosoftTeams/assign-teams-licenses).
- Provjerite je li korisniku dodijeljen [plan poziva](https://docs.microsoft.com/MicrosoftTeams/calling-plan-landing-page) .
- Omogućite korisnike za [Enterprise Voice](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/skypeforbusiness/skype-for-business-hybrid-solutions/plan-your-phone-system-cloud-pbx-solution/enable-users-for-enterprise-voice-online-and-phone-system-voicemail#to-enable-your-users-for-phone-system-in-office-365-voice-and-voicemail).
